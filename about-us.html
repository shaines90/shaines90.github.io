About LPUtalk

LPUTalk is an integrated platform that gives voice to graduates of the Lyceum of the Philippines (LPU) looking for employment. LPUTalk allows LPU graduates to gain more exposure from some of the top employers in the Philippines, while also allowing said employers a more efficient method of candidate screening by not only providing access to a pool of fresh graduates from one of the best universities in the country, but also maximizing the efficacy of the screening process through Talkpush’s proprietary automated phone screening process.

LPUTalk is a joint initiative between Talkpush Limited and the Center for Career Services and Industry Relations (CCSIR) of LPU that aims to bolster the CCSIR’s mission of connecting LPU graduates to in-demand job opportunities in the country.

In the current landscape of the Philippines’ modern industries, the graduates who walk out of universities with degrees do not necessarily walk into suitable employment. Many are under or unemployed, while top employers continue to find it challenging to make their screening processes easier, smoother, and much more efficient.

LPUTalk, in cooperation with LPU’s CCSIR, seeks to improve the hiring landscape by giving LPU graduates more than a fighting chance once they obtain their diplomas by guaranteeing that their voices are not drowned out by the crowd, or by inefficient screening procedures, or by the simple yet increasingly significant gap between a good resume and a good candidate.

LPUTalk offers LPU graduates more than a name and a resume – it gives them an actual voice that employers can listen to and screen, restoring the human factor within the hiring process that is often obfuscated by test scores, poorly developed resumes, and even unconscious biases. LPUTalk understands that employers are not looking for good test-takers, they are searching for genuinely valuable candidates with the potential that they need to hear to appreciate.
